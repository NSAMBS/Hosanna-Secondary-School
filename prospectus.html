About us

Vision

To be a leading secondary school producing responsible, innovative citizens who positively impact their communities.

To offer quality, inclusive education through a conducive learning environment, dedicated staff, and strong partnerships.

Our story

Hosanna Secondary School has served the community with consistent academic results, mentorship, and strong co‑curricular programs. We value character, practical skills, and service.

Facilities

  • Science laboratories (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • ICT lab with supervised access
  • Library and reading spaces
  • Sports grounds and MDD facilities
  • Safe boarding environment (where applicable)

Academics

Curriculum

Lower Secondary (S.1–S.4) and Upper Secondary (S.5–S.6) aligned to national standards, integrating practical projects and assessments.

Departments

  • Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Agriculture
  • Humanities: History, Geography, CRE/IRE, Entrepreneurship
  • Languages: English Language & Literature
  • Mathematics & ICT

Co‑curricular

  • Sports: Football, Netball, Athletics, Volleyball
  • Clubs: Debate, Scripture Union, Science, Red Cross, MDD
  • Leadership: Prefects’ body, class captains, club leadership

Academic calendar (summary)

  • Term I: Feb–Apr (Orientation, Mid‑term tests)
  • Term II: May–Aug (Competitions, Projects, Mock exams)
  • Term III: Sep–Nov (Final exams, Awards Day)
