Admissions

We welcome new students who are ready to embrace academic excellence and good discipline. Admissions are open for Senior One, Senior Five, and transfer students in other classes, depending on available vacancies.

Admission Guidelines

  1. Obtain an application form from the school or download it from this website.
  2. Submit the completed form with the required documents (report cards, results slips, IDs).
  3. Attend the admission interview or placement test where applicable.
  4. Successful applicants receive an admission letter and joining instructions.
  5. Parents clear the required school fees before reporting.

Enrollment Procedures

New students are expected to report on the specified date with their admission letter, original academic documents, medical forms, and personal items as listed in the joining instructions. Our staff will guide parents and students through the registration and settling-in process.
