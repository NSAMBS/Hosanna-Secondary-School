School History

Hosanna Secondary School was founded in 1999 by a group of dedicated educationists with the vision of providing quality education rooted in discipline, integrity, and academic excellence.

The school started with a small student population and limited facilities. Through commitment, community support, and strong leadership, it has grown into a well-established institution offering both O-Level and A-Level education.

Today, Hosanna Secondary School stands as a respected centre of learning, producing disciplined, responsible, and academically competent students.
