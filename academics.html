Academic Programs

Hosanna Secondary School offers a broad and balanced curriculum designed to develop learners intellectually, socially, and morally. Our academic programs follow national education standards while integrating practical skills and co-curricular activities.

Curriculum Overview

  • Lower Secondary (S.1 – S.4)
  • Upper Secondary (S.5 – S.6)

Core subjects include Mathematics, English Language, Sciences, Humanities, and selected electives such as Entrepreneurship, ICT, and Fine Art.

Departments

Science Department

Handles Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Agriculture with well-equipped laboratories and experienced teachers.

Humanities Department

Offers History, Geography, CRE/IRE, and Entrepreneurship focusing on critical thinking and real-life applications.

Languages Department

English Language and Literature in English, plus local and foreign language options where applicable.

Mathematics & ICT Department

Ensures numeracy, problem-solving skills, and digital literacy through Mathematics and ICT.

Co-curricular Activities

  • Sports: Football, Netball, Athletics, Volleyball
  • Clubs & Societies: Debate Club, Scripture Union, Science Club, Red Cross, Music Dance & Drama
  • Leadership: Prefects’ body, class captains, and club leadership
